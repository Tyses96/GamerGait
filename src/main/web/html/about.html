About GamerGait.com

Profiles

Create your Gamer Gait account to be able to leave reviews for games. Each profile will have the following statistics assosiated

  • Gaits - These are the currency of the website. Each review earns 10. Use them to make upgrades to your account.
  • Total reviews - The total amount of reviews you have left in your accounts lifetime.
  • Standing - This is a scoring system to see how good your reviews are. The higher your standing, the more reputible your reviews. Posting reviews earns standing. Downvotes decreases standing and upvotes increase standing [Still a work in progress]

Games

All games have ratings. You can leave your own rating up to a score of 100 for each category defined:

  • Graphics
  • Gameplay
  • Story
  • Value for money

The game overall rating of these categories will then change to reflect the average of all the reviews. This allows for a community driven rating. The overall rating is the average of all the individual ratings.

Free Steam Keys

On a monthly basis, free steam keys will be given out at random to people who have entered the Gait Pool. The games given out are the ones on the "Monthly top picks" section. To enter the Gait pool, you first need a verified email. Then purchase a ticket by going to your profile, and clicking "Purchase Gait Pool ticket". A ticket costs 50 gaits which is equivalent to 5 reviews. Multiple tickets can be purchased for higher odds at winning. The amount of keys distributed will be proportionate to the amount of users that have entered the Gait pool.

Reviews

Please ensure reviews are accurate and true. If found to be leaving false reviews to alter game ratings in either direction, your account and possibly your ip address, will be banned to prevent further abuse. One review per user per game!
